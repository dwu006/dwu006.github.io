Track

2023 Season

This was my first season of track and field. At first I did it because I needed to fullfill my PE credits and since my regular sport, badminton, wasn't offered at my school, so I decided to choose track. It would help me improve my stamina on the court and many of my friends were also on the team. Many people joining the team wouldn't normally go for what I think the hardest events but I still went for it and decided to do long distance as a way to get faster for badminton. As the season started, I was able to meet a lot of new people that showed so much effort and dedication to running and it made me fall in love with this sport. My first season went by so fast. The meets were so much fun- the bus rides, competing, cheering, and just hanging out. Being part of such a big and fun community was such an awesome and unforgettable experience. As the season comes to an end, I am sad that many of my senior friends I have met will be leaving, but I am also excited for cross country this fall and hopefully track next year.
