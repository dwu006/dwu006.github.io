Cross Country

2019 Season

This was my first year of Cross Country during middle school (8th grade). I joined since many of my friends were doing it and I thought it would be fun to run with them and a way for me to train for badminton too. Although I wasn't able to get on varsity because I didn't go to enough meets, I still was able to run for junior varsity and placed 4th at the championship! We also ended winning Junior Varsity Cross Country Championship for our school!
