Badminton

I have been playing badminton since I was 9 years old. It first started when we were in Taiwan visiting family, relatives, and friends. Some of my parents friends played badminton and this inspired my mom to sign up badminton classes for me back home. I first played and started learning badminton at EastBay Badminton Association. I started playing as a hobby but then I started playing competition. Due to lack of players and competition, I moved to United Badminton Club, which was a hour away. A lot of stuff happened during qurantined but in short UBC closed down everyone together to another club, Synergy, where I currently still train. There I started to improve a lot training with more and better players, and was able to place Top 16 in 2021 US Junior Nationals and Top 8 in 2022 Junior International Trials. Throughout my badminton career I have been blessed with coaches that play, train, and coach me. I would have never gotten to this point without them.
